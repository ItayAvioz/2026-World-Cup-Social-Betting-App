# ⚽ WorldCup 2026 — Predict. Compete. Get Roasted.

> The 2026 FIFA World Cup is coming. 48 teams. 104 matches. One champion.
> Who in your group actually knows football — and who just gets lucky?

---

## The idea

You and your friends all think you know best. Now prove it.

Before every game, everyone submits their predicted scoreline. The moment the whistle blows, predictions lock and reveal — you see exactly what each person picked, who went with the crowd, and who thought they knew something nobody else did.

Points stack up over 6 weeks. One leaderboard. No excuses.

---

## How it works

**Before the tournament:**
- Pick your **tournament champion** — which country lifts the trophy
- Pick the **top scorer** — who ends the tournament with the most goals
- These lock on June 11. Choose wisely.

**During the tournament:**
- Before every game, submit your predicted scoreline (e.g. Argentina 2–1 France)
- Predictions lock at kickoff — no changing your mind once the ball is rolling
- After the game, points are awarded automatically

**At kickoff:**
- Everyone's predictions go public the moment the game starts
- See the full breakdown: how many picked each team, who went for the upset, what the average predicted score was

## Your group

This isn't a public leaderboard full of strangers. You compete in **your own private group**.

One person creates the group and sends a WhatsApp invite link. Friends click, sign up, and land straight in the group. That's it.

Every group gets its own leaderboard and its own nightly AI summary — a short, funny breakdown of the day's chaos: who's climbing, who's collapsing, and who's getting publicly roasted for picking the wrong team again.

You can be in multiple groups. May the best predictor win.

---

## Nightly AI roast

Every night after the day's games, an AI writes a summary for your group.

Not a boring recap. A roast. Who climbed the table, who fell off a cliff, who had the audacity to predict that scoreline — and got it exactly right (or embarrassingly wrong).
